How to Transfer?

Steps Involved



STEP 1: Click on the Pay now Button or choose transfer Money option from menu bar, then click on the send money button.
STEP 2: Now a prompt window will be displayed asking for the email of the reciever and amount to pay.
STEP 3: Enter the E-mail of the receiver and amount to be transfered.
STEP 4: After entering the required details click on send button.
STEP 5: If you want to cancel the transaction click on close and the transaction request will be cancelled.
